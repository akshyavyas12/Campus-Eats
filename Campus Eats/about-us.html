About Campus Eats

Campus Eats is a food delivery service designed to bring fresh, delicious, and nutritious Indian vegetarian meals directly to your campus. We focus on offering a wide range of options that cater to different tastes and dietary needs. Our mission is to promote healthy eating habits with a focus on the rich flavors of Indian vegetarian cuisine.

Our Vision

We aim to revolutionize the way students and staff experience food on campus. By offering easy access to homemade-style vegetarian meals, we aim to provide students with healthy, sustainable, and affordable food options.

Our Values

  • Fresh, High-Quality Ingredients
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Health and Wellness Focus
  • Sustainability
  • Innovation in Dining
